As per the Class 1 syllabus, kids are introduced to study new subjects in Standard 1. Typically, Class 1 is the first grade which follows after kindergarten in elementary school. A child studying in this grade is usually 6-7 years old. Here’s a list of special resources that BYJU’S offers for Class 1 kids.Every students enjoy their class.

Class 2 is a very crucial stage for kids. Standard 2 is their second year in full-fledged studies. Kids undergo a tremendous transition from playful kindergarten into the world of extensive learning. At this time, it is imperative for parents to be a little more attentive to their Standard 2 kids’ learning needs.

Class 3 English curriculum is designed to enhance the language skills of kids. Children in Standard 3 have less developed written and verbal abilities. The lessons are designed in such a way that the young minds are able to grasp them well. We provide English Worksheet for Class 3 kids. These worksheets comprise puzzles, matching exercises, fill-ups, etc.
